The Number of International Student Will Not Increase Forever 
It seems that the overall number of international student in the United States has been increasing for decades. Due to this fact, some may assume that more and more international students are coming to the States and will even become a great part of American population other than original domestic citizens. However, the total number of international student will not be increasing forever, That is, the number of international students in the US will begin to drop at some time.
