Orange Chicken vs Peking Duck 
Chinese food is everywhere, even in outer space. Americans love Chinese food so much that NASA decided to send sweet and sour pork to space for their astronauts (Lee, 2008). However, what NASA thinks as Chinese food is barely recognizable to Chinese in mainland China. The truth is that most Chinese restaurants in The US serve different food from China. When I first came to US, I always joked about how bad the American Chinese food were and complained about why Chinese restaurants cook Americanized food. After I did some research and interviewed some Americans, I changed my mind. In my opinion, Chinese restaurants in US should modify the recipe most suitable for Americans. It is supposed to taste different to showcase different culture.
