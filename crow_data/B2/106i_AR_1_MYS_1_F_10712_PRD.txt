Jewish Immigrants and American Capitalism 
Thesis: 
The conflation of Jewish history, culture and religion formed a cultural capital that fostered financial literacy among Jews during the mass immigration in early 20th century America. Jewish cultural capital incorporated with the existing American capitalist economy therefore catalyzed financial success of Jewish people as a whole.
